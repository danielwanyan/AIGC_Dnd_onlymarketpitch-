Pure Marketing Pitch Rules v1 (Compressed). ASR + OCR only. EU market.

RULE: MP01 — Pure Marketing Pitch. Video contains only promotional language with zero product information.

INPUT: ASR, OCR, Comment, aigc_video_id. NEVER use Product_title.

DATA QUALITY CHECK (priority before judgment):
- ASR garbled/meaningless/fragmented → ERROR: "engineering data pollution", human review
- ASR language mismatch → ERROR: "engineering data pollution", human review
- ASR non-human language → ERROR: "engineering data pollution", human review
- ASR empty + OCR normal → proceed with OCR-only rules
- Both ASR+OCR empty → no marketing, or mark data missing
- ASR mixed with non-speech (music lyrics, noise) → flag "ASR data pollution", human review

JUDGMENT:
- Has marketing/promotional language AND zero product info → HIT (MP01)
- Has product info (features, specs, usage, materials, ingredients) → NOT hit
- Has both → NOT hit (product info present)

MARKETING KEYWORDS (EU languages):
- Price: "only", "just", "special price", "discount", "sale", "promotion", "limited time", "offer", "€", "$", "£", "buy now", "order now", "shop now", "add to cart", "click cart", "purchase"
- Scarcity: "limited stock", "last chance", "don't miss", "hurry", "selling fast", "almost gone", "while supplies last"
- FOMO: "don't regret", "miss out", "once in a lifetime", "exclusive"
- Free shipping: "free shipping", "free delivery"
- Bundles: "buy one get one", "free gift", "bonus"
- Superlatives: "best", "amazing", "incredible", "must-have", "perfect"

NOT MARKETING (product info):
- Features: "supports ANC", "waterproof", "50-hour battery", "bluetooth 5.3"
- Specs: "6.7-inch OLED", "5000mAh", "100% cotton", "vitamin C"
- Usage: "apply after cleansing", "suitable for sports", "daily use"
- Materials: "stainless steel", "organic", "natural ingredients"

AIGC CLASSIFICATION:
- aigc_video_id has value → AIGC Marketing Pitch
- aigc_video_id is NULL → Non-AIGC Marketing Pitch

SOURCE CLASSIFICATION:
- ASR has marketing + OCR empty → ASR-only
- ASR empty + OCR has marketing → OCR-only
- Both have marketing → ASR+OCR
