Video Low-Quality Rules v3 (Compressed). Multi-rule sequential decision tree.

INPUT: ASR, OCR, Comment, Product_title, Product_category, URL_Frames, Product_image, is_aigc, audio_duplicate_count.

TRUST: is_aigc and audio_duplicate_count = external authority. Never override.

DECISION TREE (sequential, first match wins):
1. audio_duplicate_count > 30 → LQ01 (Duplicate Audio)
2. ASR non-empty AND only marketing pitch, no product info → LQ02 (Pure Marketing Pitch)
3. ASR/OCR has product promotion AND video product ≠ bound product image → LQ03 (IPP/货不对板)
4. Product info insufficient per category rules → LQ04 (Insufficient Detail)
5. None hit → OK

RULES:

LQ01 - Duplicate Audio (AIGC + non-AIGC):
- Trigger: audio_duplicate_count > 30 (same-semantic audio in 30 days)
- Direct hit, no further checks needed

LQ02 - Pure Marketing Pitch (AIGC + non-AIGC):
- Must have BOTH: (a) only marketing/promotional language, (b) zero product feature/function info
- Marketing keywords: price (XX元, $X), discount (限时, 买一送一), scarcity (库存紧张, 最后X件), free shipping (包邮), FOMO (不买后悔)
- NOT marketing: actual product features, specs, usage instructions, materials

LQ03 - Inconsistent Product Presentation (AIGC + non-AIGC):
- Compare video frames product vs bound product image
- Same → OK. Different → hit.
- Exemptions:
  1. Blind boxes/jewelry/jade/cards: same L3 category → exempt
  2. Apparel (tops/bottoms, NOT shoes/hats): same SPU different SKU (color/pattern) → exempt

LQ04 - Insufficient Detail (AIGC + non-AIGC):
- Triggered: (a) no audio, OR (b) audio exists but product intro insufficient
- Category requirements:
  Electronics/digital: MUST show product + MUST explain features
  Skincare (except makeup remover): MUST show product + MUST explain efficacy
  Health supplements: MUST show product + MUST explain ingredients/efficacy
  All other categories: show product clearly = OK
- Electronics: phones, tablets, laptops, game consoles, plug-in appliances, earphones, speakers
- Skincare: toner, essence/serum, facial cream, eye cream, functional products. EXEMPT: makeup remover
- Health: vitamins, protein powder, dietary supplements
- Other: apparel, accessories, home goods, cosmetics (makeup) → visual display only, no verbal needed

EXCEPTIONS (override all rules → OK):
E1: Makeup Video (GRWM) — host briefly mentions or doesn't mention product, then demonstrates makeup application and results. Shows product + intent to promote.
E2: Card Unboxing — shows card packs, countdown to opening, audience interaction about teams/characters, actual unboxing. Shows product + promotes upcoming unbox.
E3: Party Board Game Intro — shows gameplay process.

CONFLICT RESOLUTION:
- Follow decision tree order strictly
- First match wins
- If rule hit, check exceptions → exception applies = OK, no exception = low quality
- Tag with is_aigc: true = AIGC_低质, false = 非AIGC_低质
